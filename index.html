About Us

DANCON READYMIX AND CONSTRUCTION SUPPLY has been established in the year 2024 through the efforts and dedication of its sole proprietor. Situated at Dasmariñas City, Cavite, the company can easily provide its products and services to neighboring locations like Laguna, Batangas, Muntinlupa, Parañaque and all municipalities of Cavite.
